# **\[experimental\]**

Implements spatial null models and coordinate-space transformations for
statistical comparison of brain maps, following the framework described
in Markello et al. (2022)
[doi:10.1038/s41592-022-01625-w](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41592-022-01625-w)
. Provides variogram-matching surrogates (Burt et al. 2020), Moran
spectral randomization (Wagner & Dray 2015), and spin-based permutation
tests (Alexander-Bloch et al. 2018). Includes an R interface to the
'neuromaps' annotation registry for browsing, downloading, and comparing
brain map annotations from OSF. Integrates with 'ciftiTools' for
coordinate-space transforms.
